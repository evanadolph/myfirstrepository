Strength

Boston has some of the strongest fans in all of the leagues. In New England, the temperature is radical and with 4 different seasons fans have to be willing to go through a lot to watch their favorits sports team. On January 10, 2004, the Tennessee Titans came to Gillette Stadium in Foxborough to play an AFC Divisional Playoff game. Temperatures were in the single digits. Fans had runny noses but still attended. Tailgators created fires shivering in blankets. Struggling through what felt like 10-12 below zero the stadium was almost packed. Over 68,000 fans in attendance, the Pats would won 17-14 thanks to a late 46-yard field goal by Adam Vinatieri.
